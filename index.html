Question 1

Attend an interesting discussion. Listen to a compelling lecture.

Question 3

After a large event, how do you normally feel?

Revived and refreshed. Possibly even ready for even more fun! I feel tired or drained.

Question 4

If you are home alone for a long period of time, how are you feeling?

Restless. I figure out something to do. Excited. I finally have alone time or time to relax.

Question 5

How do you react to a disagreement?

I let out my thoughts right away. I also want to resolve the issue immediately. I need time to think and process before I can speak up.

Question 6

Hesitant. I have to plan my conversations ahead of time. Ready to talk to anyone! Thinking of new topics to talk about comes naturally.
